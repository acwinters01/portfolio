Our Philosophy

"At Mythic Echo Interactive, we believe in the transformative power of games. Our philosophy is to create immersive experiences that not only entertain but also inspire self-discovery and reflection. We embrace diversity in all forms, weaving complex narratives that explore the metaphysical aspects of life and the untapped potential within every individual."

"Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that games can be a powerful medium for exploring deeper truths about ourselves and the universe. We create RPGs that challenge conventional narratives, offering players a chance to explore alternate realities, question the nature of existence, and unlock their own divine potential. At Mythic Echo Interactive, we are committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in gaming."

Our Projects

Stellar Ascension (2019)

Genre: Fantasy/Sci-Fi RPG

"Stellar Ascension" is an epic fantasy/sci-fi RPG that takes players on a journey across a vast and mysterious universe. In Stellar Ascension, players take on the role of a protagonist who awakens with no memory of their past but with an innate connection to the cosmos. They soon discover that they are a Celestial Traveler, an individual chosen to restore balance to the universe by awakening ancient powers within themselves and others. Players must navigate a universe in turmoil, where different factions vie for control over powerful cosmic energies that could either save or destroy entire worlds.

Echoes of the Veil (2021)

Genre: Dark Fantasy RPG

"Echoes of the Veil" is a dark fantasy RPG that explores themes of mystery, the supernatural, and the boundaries between different realities. The game is set in a world where the Veil, a mystical barrier separating the material world from the realm of spirits and ancient powers, is thinning.

Players take on the role of a Seeker, an individual with the rare ability to sense and interact with the Veil. The protagonist's journey begins when their village is attacked by the Echoes, leaving them as the sole survivor. Determined to uncover the truth behind the Echoes and the weakening of the Veil, the Seeker sets out on a quest to explore the dark and twisted forests, ancient ruins, and forgotten temples.

Awakening the Divine (2022)

Genre: RPG

"Awakening the Divine" is set in a fantastical multiverse where each world represents different elements of the human spirit and the universe's hidden truths. These worlds are interconnected by ancient, mystical pathways that only the chosen can traverse.

Players assume the role of an individual known as the "Pilgrim," who is prophesied to have the potential to awaken the divine powers within. The Pilgrim embarks on a journey through diverse, richly detailed realms to uncover their true nature and purpose. Along the way, they must confront both external adversaries and internal conflicts, each symbolizing the struggle for self-discovery and enlightenment.

Reflections of the Infinite (2023)

Genre: Science-Fantasy RPG

"Reflections of the Infinite" takes place in a mesmerizing, multidimensional universe called the Astral Nexus. This universe is composed of interconnected realms, each reflecting different aspects of existence, consciousness, and reality. These realms are visually stunning, with surreal landscapes that blend both organic and artificial elements.

Players take on the role of an individual known as the 'Echo,' a being with the unique ability to traverse between dimensions and interact with the fundamental forces of the universe. The Echo's journey begins when a cosmic anomaly disrupts the balance of the Astral Nexus, threatening to collapse the delicate equilibrium of all realms. The Echo must uncover the source of this disturbance, restore balance, and unlock the secrets of their own existence.

Fragments of the Self (2023)

Genre: Psychological Sci-Fi RPG

"Fragments of the Self" is set in a near-future world where space exploration has reached new heights. Players journey through both the vast expanse of outer space and the complex inner landscapes of the human mind. The primary setting is a mysterious space station orbiting a distant, enigmatic planet, and the surreal, dream-like environments within the protagonist's psyche.

Players assume the role of an astronaut named Dr. Alex Mercer, who is part of a deep-space mission to study an unknown celestial phenomenon near the planet Iris. After a catastrophic event, Alex finds themselves stranded on the space station with no contact with Earth and an array of hauntingly lifelike androids. As Alex explores the station and encounters these androids, they must also delve into their fragmented memories and subconscious mind to uncover the truth about the mission, the phenomenon, and their own identity.

Synthetic Dusk (2024)

Genre: Cyberpunk Action RPG

"Synthetic Dusk" is set in a sprawling, neon-lit metropolis known as Eclipsia, where towering skyscrapers and dark alleys form a stark contrast. The city is a hub of advanced technology, corporate espionage, and hidden underworld activities. The boundaries between the digital and physical worlds are blurred, creating a society where artificial intelligence and cybernetic enhancements are commonplace.

Players take on the role of Riley, a former cybernetics engineer turned rogue operative, who is on a mission to uncover the truth behind a mysterious phenomenon known as the "Synthetic Dusk." This event is causing unexplained anomalies within the city's AI systems and cybernetic networks, leading to chaos and danger. As Riley delves deeper, they uncover a conspiracy that threatens not only Eclipsia but the very fabric of reality itself.
