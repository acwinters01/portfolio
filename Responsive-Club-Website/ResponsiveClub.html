About St. Louis SC

The St. Louis Skating Club is the perfect place for anyone who loves figure skating.

With a rich history spanning nearly 130 years, the club is home to some of the most passionate skaters in the Midwest. Whether you're a beginner just starting out, or an experienced skater looking to take your skills to the next level, the St. Louis Skating Club has something to offer.

Since 1932

Member of U.S. Figure Skating

Established in 1984 and subsequent memeership in U.S. Figure Skating since 1932, St. Louis Skating Club boasts a longstanding legacy of fostering and contributing to the growth of figure skating in St. Louis.
